Wet Zone

Slip n’ slide your way through Sri Lanka’s first American standard waterpark! We’ve got fiveteen fun and thrilling slides for all ages including four pools. Whether you're looking for a fun family outing, a thrilling adventure with friends, or just a relaxing day in the sun, this water slides are the perfect destination. So come on in, the water's fine! Whatever your age or preference, water slides are sure to provide a fun and memorable experience at ceyloland wet zone. So don't forget to bring your swimsuit, sunscreen, and a sense of adventure on your visit!

>>>Proper Swimwear or Nylone shorts t shirts are mandatory for those who are inerested in using Wet Zone.

  • The Twister
  • Topsy Turvy
  • Splasher
  • Curly Wurly
  • Faster Master
  • Kiddies Pool
  • Wave Pool
  • Ladies Pool
  • Crazy Pool
